# Scheduling Recorded Video Interviews

Modern hiring has evolved beyond in-person interviews. As companies grow globally, they need better ways to connect with candidates regardless of where candidates are located.

Zoho Recruit's Recorded Video Interview offers a human-centered approach to remote hiring that works for both companies and candidates. Here's how it helps teams evaluate talent more effectively:

- Assess candidates thoroughly regardless of location
- Maintain consistent interview processes with standardised questions
- Evaluate responses thoughtfully at their convenience
- Accommodate global time zones and distributed hiring teams

## Availability

| | |
|---|---|

| **Plans** | Enterprise, People Plus, Zoho One |

## How It Works

Recorded Video Interviews allow you to create a standardized set of questions that candidates can answer through pre-recorded video responses. Unlike live video interviews, this asynchronous approach offers several distinct advantages:

1. You can review and analyze responses multiple times
2. Hiring teams can collaborate more effectively on candidate evaluation
3. Candidates can showcase their best responses without time zone constraints
4. The process ensures equal treatment of all candidates through consistent questioning

## Scheduling Recorded Video Interviews

### Via Interview Module

1. Navigate to the **Interviews** module and click on the **+** icon (Create).
2. Select **Video Interview** from the interview type pop-up.
3. Select **Video Interview > Recorded Video Interview**.
4. The **Create Interview** window will appear. Enter the required interview details such as:
   - Deadline
   - Posting Title
   - Candidate Name
   - Interviewer(s)
   - Assessment Name
5. Click **Schedule** to confirm.

Once the interview is scheduled, the **candidate will receive a link via email** with instructions to complete their one-way video interview.

---

### Via Candidates Module

1. Access the **Candidates** module, then select a candidate record.
2. Click **Schedule Interview** for the desired candidate.
3. Select **Video Interview > Recorded Video Interview**. Fill in the required details:
   - Deadline
   - Posting Title
   - Candidate Name
   - Interviewer(s)
   - Assessment Name
4. Click **Schedule** to confirm.

> **Note:**
> - Recorded Interviews can be scheduled only if the candidate record has an email address.
> - Candidates will receive the link to the interview via email.

---

## Post-Scheduling Actions

All participants receive automatic email notifications. The email includes:

1. Video conference link
2. Date and time details
3. Calendar invitation
4. Any additional instructions

---

## Managing Scheduled Interviews

### Rescheduling Process

1. Navigate to the **Interview** module and choose the **interview record** you want to reschedule.
2. Click **Reschedule**.
3. Update the details and click **Reschedule**.

> **Note:** An alternative email with edited details will be sent to candidates.

### Cancellation Process

1. Navigate to the **Interview** module and choose the interview record you want to cancel.
2. Click the menu in the top-right corner, then select **Cancel Interview**.
3. Provide a cancellation **reason** and **feedback** (optional).
4. Confirm **cancellation** to notify all participants.

## Video Interview Credits

Video interviews operate using video credits. By default, one video interview credit is provided. A single credit allows multiple video interviews for one active job opening.

To obtain additional video interview credits, you can purchase an add-on for **$12 or ₹600 per month per credit** under:

> **Profile > Manage Subscription > Upgrade Users/Add-ons**

For more details on video interview credits, visit the FAQ page on Video Interviews.
